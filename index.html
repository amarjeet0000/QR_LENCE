QR Lens – QR Code and Barcode Scanner App

QR Lens is a modern QR code and barcode scanner and generator app. Scan QR codes, barcodes, product codes and generate QR codes quickly, securely and without sign-up.

About the App

QR Lens is designed for speed, simplicity and privacy. The app allows users to scan QR codes and barcodes in real time, open links instantly and generate QR codes for text and URLs. All scanning is processed locally on your device.

Key Features

Fast QR Scanner

Instant QR code scanning with high accuracy.

Barcode Reader

Scan product barcodes and get information.

QR Code Generator

Create QR codes for text, links and more.

Privacy First

No login. No signup. Local data storage only.

Privacy & Permissions

  • Camera permission is used only for scanning QR codes
  • Internet access is used to open scanned links and show ads
  • No personal data is collected by the developer
  • No account or login required
